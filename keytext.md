## Description
SnapShift is the simplest way to track your body transformation through progress photos. Whether you are building muscle, losing weight, or working toward any fitness goal, SnapShift keeps you motivated by showing real visual change over time.

WHY SNAPSHOT?

The scale does not tell the whole story. Photos reveal what numbers cannot. SnapShift makes it effortless to capture consistent progress photos and compare them side by side so you can see how far you have come.

KEY FEATURES

Ghost Overlay Camera
Align every photo perfectly. A transparent outline of your previous photo appears on screen so each new shot matches the last. No more guessing angles or poses.

Three Comparison Modes
Compare your photos the way you want. Use side-by-side view, a swipeable slider, or an overlay blend to spot every detail of your progress.

Timelapse Videos
Turn your progress photos into a smooth timelapse video. Watch weeks or months of transformation in seconds and share it with friends or on social media.

Body Metrics Tracking
Log your weight and body fat percentage alongside each photo. Keep all your progress data in one place for the complete picture.

Voice Activated Shutter
Hands full? Just say the word. Voice-activated shutter lets you take photos without touching your phone.

Privacy First
Your photos stay on your device. Face ID and Touch ID lock keeps your progress private. Export with automatic privacy blur to hide faces when sharing.

iCloud Sync
Your albums and photos sync seamlessly across all your Apple devices via iCloud.

Batch Import
Already have progress photos in your camera roll? Import them all at once and organize by date.
